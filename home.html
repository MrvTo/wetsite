About W.E.T

Webcam-based eye tracking software for research and development

What is W.E.T?

W.E.T (Webcam Eye Tracking) is a software application that enables eye movement tracking using standard webcams. The software uses computer vision algorithms to estimate gaze direction and eye movements, making eye tracking research more accessible and cost-effective.

Our Goal

We aim to provide researchers, developers, and students with an affordable alternative to expensive eye tracking hardware. While not as precise as specialized equipment, W.E.T offers a practical solution for many research applications and educational purposes.
